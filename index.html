Academic Programs

At Greenwood International, we pride ourselves on a robust academic curriculum that caters to the individual needs of each student. Our programs are                                                    meticulously designed to foster critical thinking, creativity, and a love for lifelong learning.

1. STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics)

Advanced Laboratories:

Our state-of-the-art science and computer labs provide students with hands-on experience in cutting-edge technology and scientific research.

Innovative Projects:

Students engage in projects such as robotics, coding, and environmental science, which are presented in national and international competitions.

3. Arts and Creative Studies

Visual Arts: Our art studios are equipped with modern facilities where students can explore painting, sculpture, and digital art.

Performing Arts:

With a focus on drama, music, and dance, students have numerous opportunities to showcase their talents in school productions and community events.
